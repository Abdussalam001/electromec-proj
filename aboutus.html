Welcome to Global Electromech Power Limited a subsidiary of Onwardglobal logistics solution concept established in 2015 with the objective of carrying out distribution, Engineering, Sales Hire, Leasing, Servicing, Maintenance, Overhauling and rentals of diesel generating sets contributing to the development of power sector in Nigeria economy.
